About Me

The tech community has some amazing people.  

There are challenges in coding.   But video games have challenges too and I don’t stop playing them because there’s challenges.   To me, coding is like a never ending video game. So many possibilities when it comes coding and the tech field as a whole.

I've also been a chemist working several years in a laboratory.   I've enjoyed mixing chemicals and have learned so much. But coding is just more fun.

In my free time, I run with my dog, hiking, video games, read books at coffee shops, and play Pokemon Go.
